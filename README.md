# Superstore Sales Analysis – Excel

Sales and profitability analysis of the Superstore dataset using MySQL. Covers category performance, regional trends, customer segments, discount analysis, and year-over-year growth.

## Project Idea

I used a Superstore sales dataset from Kaggle (around 9.8k rows).  
Goal: understand **which regions, states and product categories** bring more sales.

## What I did

- Imported the dataset into Excel
- Checked basic data quality (types, blanks, duplicates)
- Built a **clean table** for analysis
- Used PivotTables to create:
  - Sales by Region
  - Sales by Category
  - Sales by State (Top states)
  - Monthly Sales trend
- Designed a simple dashboard page with charts and KPI cards

## Main Results

- Total sales are around **2.26M** (in the dataset)
- **West** region is the strongest by revenue
- **California** is the top state by sales
- Technology is the best performing category
- Sales show an **overall increasing trend** over the years
